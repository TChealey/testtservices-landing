What We Do

Test T Services LLC provides last-mile delivery and routed logistics for healthcare, retail, and business clients. We support scheduled routes, on-demand pickups, same-day delivery, and STAT coverage by request.

Last-Mile Service Lines

Medical & Healthcare Delivery

  • Lab specimens, blood, medical supplies, and equipment transport
  • Professional handling with chain-of-custody discipline
  • HIPAA- and BBP-conscious operations

Organ & Tissue Courier

  • Urgent, time-critical deliveries and direct hand-offs
  • Temperature-sensitive and STAT handling by request

Pharmaceutical Delivery

  • Secure pickup and delivery of medications and prescriptions
  • Scheduled runs and on-demand options

Retail Last-Mile Delivery

  • Store-to-door delivery, same-day and scheduled drop-offs
  • Return logistics and local redistribution

Documents & Business Courier

  • Confidential document handling for medical, legal, and business clients
  • Time-sensitive drop-offs with proof of delivery

B2B Local Express

  • Parts, packages, and critical items requiring reliable last-mile delivery
  • Same-day and routed support for local businesses
